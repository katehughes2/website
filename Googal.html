O-stretching my wings

first ostrich flight takes place

On July 2nd 2017, the first ostrich flight took place at an undiscolsed location. Officials refuse to disclose the location of this flight, but have offered some details which are as follows

  • The ostrich was a juvenile female called Penelope
  • The ostrich suffered no harm during take off and landing
  • The ostrich flew approximately 200 metres on a sunny day

This is a breaking news story and we are bringing you updates as soon as we receive them from the National Ostrich Centre!
